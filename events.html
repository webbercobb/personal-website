Alexa Truth or Dare — Debut Solo Exhibition

December 11, 2025 – January 20, 2026
545 West 23rd Street, New York, NY

Alexa Truth or Dare is Connor Wright's debut solo exhibition, presenting monumental oil-stick and spray-paint paintings on linen that are at once incantatory, architectural, and devotional. Across large-scale canvases, the exhibition builds an immersive environment that explores image overload, memory, and contemporary myth-making.

Artist Talk: Connor Wright & Harrison Ford (ARTnews)

Tuesday, December 16, 2025
545 West 23rd Street, New York, NY

Artist Connor Wright joins Harrison Ford, Digital Director at ARTnews, for an in-depth conversation inside the exhibition Alexa Truth or Dare. Together they will discuss the making of the show, its references and imagery, and the role of painting in an age of algorithmic images and constant visual saturation.
